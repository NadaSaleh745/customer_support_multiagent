# Frequently Asked Questions

## General
**Q: Where is my order?**
A: You can track your order using your Order ID. The Order Tracking Agent can provide real-time updates.

**Q: How do I change my shipping address?**
A: If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact support to update your address. If it has shipped, you may need to contact the carrier.

**Q: My product arrived damaged. What should I do?**
A: Please contact our Complaint Agent. We will offer a replacement or a full refund for damaged items.

## Payments
**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

**Q: When will I receive my refund?**
A: Once approved, refunds typically take 5-7 business days to appear in your account.

## Products
**Q: Are your products covered by warranty?**
A: Yes, all electronic products come with a 1-year limited warranty.

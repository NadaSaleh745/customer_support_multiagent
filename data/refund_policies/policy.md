# Refund Policy

## Eligibility
- Items must be returned within 30 days of the purchase date.
- Items must be in their original condition and packaging.
- Damaged or defective items are eligible for a full refund or replacement.

## Process
1. Customers must provide an order ID and the reason for the refund.
2. The Refund Agent will verify the purchase date and eligibility.
3. If eligible, the refund will be processed to the original payment method.

## Exceptions
- Software, gift cards, and personalized items are non-refundable.
- Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the item arrived damaged.

## Cancellation Policy
- Orders can be cancelled if they have not yet been shipped.
- Once an order has reached "Shipped" status, it must follow the standard refund process.
